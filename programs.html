Results-Driven Dog Training

Whether you have a wild puppy or a highly reactive adult dog, our tailored Board & Train programs are designed to reset habits entirely and return a happy, balanced dog to your home.

Select Your Track

Puppy Foundations (14 Days)

For dogs under 6 months. Focuses on socialization, crate training, potty schedule, and basic marker training.

  • Potty Training basics
  • Bite inhibition
  • Crate acceptance

Standard B&T (21 Days)

For adult dogs. Resolves pulling, jumping, ignoring commands, counter-surfing, and mild reactivity.

  • On-leash perfection
  • Place command duration
  • Public proofing

Rehab Track (30+ Days)

For dogs with severe aggression resource guarding, or deep anxiety issues requiring extensive behavior modification.

  • Aggression desensitization
  • Emotional regulation
  • muzzle conditioning

A Day in the Life

Structure breeds calmness. While at our facility, your dog will follow a strict, predictable schedule that reduces anxiety and maximizes learning.

7:00 AM
Potty break, breakfast, and digestion rest.
9:00 AM
Session 1: New concepts & individual obedience training.
12:00 PM
Structured play, treadmill workout, and mid-day rest.
3:00 PM
Session 2: Real-world proofing (parks, stores).
6:00 PM
Dinner, light grooming, and final review.
