Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We have answers. If you don't see your question here, feel free to reach out.

General & Facility

Can I visit my dog during Board & Train?

Visits disrupt the dog's mindset and break the psychological reset process. We do not allow visits during the first 2 weeks, but you will receive daily updates on your dashboard.

What vaccinations are required?

All dogs must have up-to-date Rabies, DHPP, and Bordetella vaccines. Proof of vaccination is required before check-in.

Training & Methodology

Do you use E-Collars?

Yes, for adult Board & Train. We use top-of-the-line communicators to act as an invisible leash. They are never used as punishment, but as a tapping communication tool.

What if my dog reverts back to old habits?

Dogs do not revert unless owners revert. We provide extensive go-home transfer sessions to train YOU on how to maintain the training. If you follow the rules and struggle, our Lifetime Support guarantee covers free group follow-ups.
