What is Board & Train?

The fastest, most effective way to train your dog. Your dog stays with our professional trainers 24/7, learning through repetition and consistency in a structured environment.

Immersive Learning

Your dog lives at our premium facility, completely removed from bad habits and environmental triggers allowing for a complete reset.

Rapid Results

Professional trainers spend hundreds of hours reinforcing good behavior, achieving results in 3 weeks what might take owners a year.

Daily Updates

Balanced, Real-World Training Methods

We don't just teach tricks in a classroom. We train dogs to be well-behaved family members everywhere you go.

  • Positive Reinforcement First: We build motivation and confidence using rewards your dog loves.
  • Clear Communication: Creating a language between you and your dog that eliminates confusion.
  • Real-World Proofing: We train in parks, stores, and crowds so your dog listens amidst distractions.
