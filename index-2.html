The 21-Day Transformation

Our structured timeline guarantees results through methodical, step-by-step advancement in distraction levels.

Week 1: Foundation & Focus

We establish clear communication and core obedience commands (Sit, Down, Place, Heel, Recall) inside our low-distraction indoor facility.

Week 2: Proofing & Distraction

Commands are tested in our outdoor training yards around other dogs, noises, and movement to build reliability under pressure.

Week 3: Real-World Application

Field trips to parks, home improvement stores, and busy trails ensure the training transfers to your actual lifestyle. Followed by owner transfer sessions.
